Research Interest

I work in the field of remote sensing image quality improvement, low-level vision, multi-source remote sensing information fusion and deep learning theory. I focus on the following research topics:
  • Thick cloud removal in multi-temporal data
  • Time-series production reconstruction
  • Hyperspectral image denoising
  • SAR image despeckling
